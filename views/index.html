About the novel

Romance of the Three Kingdoms is a historical novel written in the 14th century by Luo Guanzhong. It is one of the Four Great Classical Novels of China, and the basis of Koei's popular video game franchises Romance of the Three Kingdoms and Dynasty Warriors.

Don't just read it, see it

The novel contains 120 chapters and around 1000 named characters. Hundreds of characters from the novel appear as officers in the Koei games. The number of characters and the complexity of their relationships makes visualization a great tool to help the reader or student understand the novel and its various adaptations.
